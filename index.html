Path to Programming

My interest in programming:

One of my friends has a job in cybersecurity and is the one who originally piqued my interest in programming, and while I don't have very much experience in programming, I find the applications of it very interesting I am also pretty creative, but unfortunately I don't have very much artistic talent as far as drawing, or painting goes, but it seems like programming is another extension of artistic creation, and one that you don't have to necessarily have inherent skill in as long as you put in the time and dedication (although I'm sure much of the same could be said for drawing and painting). Another reason I would be interested in programming other than the creative outlet I hope I can turn it into, is I have a desire to feel passion in my career, and I believe that web development could be something that inspires me in my day to day life and gives me a feeling of fufillment, which I believe anyone seeks in their career. It is an expanding job market, and no matter what else the future seems geared towards people who have knowledge in this sector.
